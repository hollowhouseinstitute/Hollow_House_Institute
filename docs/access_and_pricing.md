# Access and Pricing

## Overview

The Hollow House Institute provides tiered access to datasets and research materials to support ethical research while sustaining our operations. This document outlines our access tiers, pricing structure, and application process.

## Access Tiers

### Tier 1: Academic Research (Free)

**Eligibility:**
- Faculty, postdoctoral researchers, and graduate students at accredited institutions
- Non-profit research organizations
- Independent researchers working on non-commercial projects

**Access Includes:**
- Full access to open datasets
- Standard documentation and metadata
- Community support forum access
- Monthly usage limits apply

**Application Process:**
- Complete [Researcher Access Application](./researcher_access_application.md)
- Provide institutional verification
- Review time: 5-7 business days

### Tier 2: Enhanced Research ($500/year)

**Eligibility:**
- All Tier 1 researchers seeking enhanced access
- Small research teams (up to 5 members)

**Access Includes:**
- All Tier 1 benefits
- Priority technical support
- Access to pre-release datasets (beta)
- Increased usage limits
- Quarterly office hours with research team

### Tier 3: Enterprise ($5,000+/year)

**Eligibility:**
- Commercial entities
- Large research organizations
- Custom research partnerships

**Access Includes:**
- All Tier 2 benefits
- Commercial usage rights
- Custom data extracts and formats
- Dedicated technical support
- Service level agreements (SLA)
- White-label options available

**Pricing:**
- Base: $5,000/year
- Custom pricing for large-scale deployments
- Contact enterprise@hollowhouseinstitute.org

## Application Process

### Step 1: Review Documentation
- Read [AI Safety Policy](./ai_safety_policy.md)
- Review [Ethics and Limits](./ethics_and_limits.md)
- Understand [Data Broker Disclosure](./data_broker_disclosure.md)

### Step 2: Complete Application
- Fill out the [Researcher Access Application](./researcher_access_application.md)
- Provide necessary verification documents
- Describe intended use case

### Step 3: Review and Approval
- Our team reviews all applications
- We may request additional information
- Timeline: 5-7 business days for standard applications
- Enterprise applications: 2-3 weeks

### Step 4: Onboarding
- Accept license agreement
- Receive access credentials
- Complete brief orientation (Enterprise tier)

## Pricing Justification

Our pricing structure supports:
- Ongoing participant anonymization and privacy protection
- Dataset maintenance and quality assurance
- Infrastructure costs for secure data hosting
- Community support and documentation
- Continued research and dataset expansion

## Financial Assistance

We understand that funding constraints can limit research access. We offer:
- Sliding scale pricing for qualifying researchers
- Payment plans for multi-year access
- Grant support letters for funding applications
- Limited fee waivers for exceptional circumstances

Contact access@hollowhouseinstitute.org to discuss options.

## Renewal and Cancellation

- Annual licenses auto-renew unless cancelled
- 30-day cancellation notice required
- Pro-rated refunds not available
- Grace period: 15 days after expiration

## Usage Monitoring

All access tiers include usage monitoring to:
- Ensure compliance with license terms
- Prevent abuse and unauthorized sharing
- Plan infrastructure scaling
- Improve service quality

We do not monitor research content, only access patterns and usage volume.

## Questions

For questions about access and pricing:
- General inquiries: access@hollowhouseinstitute.org
- Enterprise inquiries: enterprise@hollowhouseinstitute.org
- Technical support: support@hollowhouseinstitute.org
