## Submission Instructions

### How to Submit

**Email Submission**:
Send completed application to: applications@hollowhouseinstitute.org
- Subject line: "Dataset Access Application - [Your Name]"
- Attach all required documents as PDFs
- Maximum attachment size: 10MB

**Online Portal** (coming soon):
Applications will be accepted through our online portal at hollowhouseinstitute.org/apply

### Application Review Process

1. **Receipt Confirmation**: You'll receive confirmation within 1-2 business days
2. **Initial Review**: Our team reviews for completeness (2-3 days)
3. **Detailed Evaluation**: Research and ethics review (3-5 days)
4. **Clarifications**: We may request additional information
5. **Decision**: You'll receive approval or denial with explanation
6. **Onboarding**: Approved applicants receive access credentials

**Total Timeline**: 5-7 business days for standard applications; 2-3 weeks for enterprise applications

### Review Criteria

Applications are evaluated on:
- Clarity and feasibility of research plan
- Ethical considerations and safeguards
- Alignment with our mission and policies
- Institutional credentials and support
- Data security and handling procedures
- Previous research track record (where applicable)

### Approval Outcomes

**Approved**: 
- Receive access credentials
- License agreement signature required
- Onboarding materials provided

**Conditional Approval**:
- Requires specific modifications or additional documentation
- Must address conditions within 30 days

**Denied**:
- Explanation provided
- May reapply after addressing concerns
- Appeal process available

## Appeals Process

If your application is denied:
1. Email appeals@hollowhouseinstitute.org within 30 days
2. Explain why you believe the decision should be reconsidered
3. Provide additional supporting information
4. Appeals reviewed within 14 days

## Renewal

Annual renewal required for all access tiers:
- 30 days before expiration
- Update research progress
- Confirm continued compliance
- Simplified renewal form for existing users
