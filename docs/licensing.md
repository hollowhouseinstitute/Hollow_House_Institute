# Licensing Overview

## Introduction

The Hollow House Institute is committed to providing open, ethical, and trauma-informed access to datasets and research materials. This document outlines our licensing framework and provides links to specific dataset licenses.

## Core Licensing Principles

1. **Open Access with Responsibility**: Our datasets are licensed to promote research while protecting participant privacy and preventing harmful uses.

2. **Trauma-Informed Approach**: All licensing terms consider the sensitive nature of our data and prioritize the wellbeing of research participants.

3. **Clear Usage Guidelines**: Each dataset includes explicit usage restrictions and ethical guidelines.

## Dataset License Types

### Research License
- Intended for academic and non-profit research
- No commercial use without separate agreement
- Attribution required
- Sharing restrictions apply

### Enterprise License
- Commercial use permitted under specific terms
- See [Enterprise Terms of Service](./enterprise_terms_of_service.md)
- Enhanced support and documentation included
- Custom agreements available

### Educational License
- For accredited educational institutions
- Teaching and curriculum development permitted
- Attribution required
- No redistribution

## License Files Location

Individual dataset licenses are located in the `dataset_license/` directory at the repository root. Each dataset maintains its own license file that includes:

- Specific usage restrictions
- Attribution requirements
- Redistribution terms
- Contact information for questions

## Applying for Access

To request access to licensed datasets:

1. Review the [Access and Pricing](./access_and_pricing.md) documentation
2. Complete the [Researcher Access Application](./researcher_access_application.md)
3. Wait for review and approval (typically 5-7 business days)
4. Accept the license agreement upon approval

## Questions and Support

For licensing questions, contact:
- Email: licensing@hollowhouseinstitute.org
- For commercial licensing inquiries: enterprise@hollowhouseinstitute.org

## Version History

This licensing framework is subject to updates. Major changes will be communicated to all current license holders.
